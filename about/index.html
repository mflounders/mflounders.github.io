Research

My research is focused on understanding the brain with non-invasive multi-modal imaging. During my post-baccalaureate fellowship at the NIH with Biyu He, I studied visual perception using fMRI and MEG methods and multivariate pattern analysis. Recently, I have applied neuroimaging analysis in clinical settings to develop individualized treatment for depression under the mentorship of Desmond Oathes at UPenn.
